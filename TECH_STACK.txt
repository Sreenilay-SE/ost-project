  └─ Knowledge Sphere:

  └─ 4 Floating Orbital Nodes (About/Activities/Resources/Connect):

       • 6 SphereGeometry satellite dots with orbital animation
  └─ PlaneGeometry background architectural grid (wireframe)
  └─ 300 BufferGeometry ambient particles

  Lighting Rig (Studio Setup):
  └─ AmbientLight (warm white, intensity 5.0)
  └─ DirectionalLight key light (warm, intensity 5.0 + shadows)
  └─ DirectionalLight fill light (cool blue, intensity 3.5)
  └─ PointLight gold accent (animated, orbiting)
  └─ PointLight silver accent (animated, orbiting)

  Camera Physics:
  └─ Magnetic cursor drift: lerp(currentPos, target + mouseOffset)
  └─ Fly-to animation: smooth z/x/y lerp over 1.4 seconds
  └─ Exponential fog: FogExp2 for infinite-scale depth illusion
